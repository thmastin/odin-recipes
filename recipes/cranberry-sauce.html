Cranberry Sauce

Description

A must have side to go with turkey for every occasion. You could always by the gelatinous goop that is sauce in a can, but why would you when you can easily make it yourself and it is so much more tasty!

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cranberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice

Steps

  1. Dissolve sugar in the orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. Stir in cranberries and cook until they start to pop, about 10 minutes
  3. Remove from heat and place sauce ina a bowl. It will thicken as it cools
